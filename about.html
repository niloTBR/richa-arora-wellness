My Story

Rooted in Science. Guided by Nature. Proven by Results.

I am a Naturopath (BNYS) and Ayurvedic Practitioner registered with the STED Council, Government of India, with over 12 years of expertise in Integrative Wellness, Natural Healing Methods & Alternative Medical Sciences. At the core of everything I do is a firm belief: popping pills and lifelong dependence on medicines are not the cure for any illness. The body can heal itself - when we address what is actually causing it to break down.

"My goal is to treat the person - not the disease, not the system, not just the symptoms."

Through personalized integrative consultations, my team and I diagnose the root cause of your discomfort by mapping every parameter of the human body - top to toe - tracing the timeline from childhood to the present. We integrate the best protocols from alternative sciences to create a blueprint that uniquely works for you. Whether you have a lifestyle condition, a medical ailment, or simply want to elevate your quality of life, we are fully dedicated to guiding you as a partner in your wellness journey.

We recognize that each individual's body is unique. Our curated programs - ranging from 21 days to 12 weeks - are customized for every individual with one common goal: to live a wholesome, healthy, and medicine-free life. We work with children, adolescents, and adults alike, and our commitment is to pull you out of the vicious circle of medicines and their side effects - for good.

The Journey

Milestones That Shaped My Practice

Every step of my journey has contributed to the integrative approach I use today to help clients achieve lasting wellness.

2008

The Foundation - THAC

Began as a Wellness Officer at The Health Awareness Center (THAC), working under the direct mentorship of Dr. Vijaya Venkat - a pioneering figure in natural health, nutrition science, and drug-free living in India. This formative experience became the philosophical bedrock of everything that followed.

2014

Ayurveda Training, Kerala

Embarked on an intensive immersion in classical Ayurvedic medicine in Kerala, learning directly from traditional practitioners and masters. This deepened an understanding of the body's constitutions, seasonal rhythms, and the ancient art of restoring balance from within.

2016

BNYS - Naturopathy & Yogic Sciences

Began the Bachelor of Naturopathy & Yogic Sciences (BNYS) programme - a rigorous multi-year course formally merging natural healing, functional nutrition, and yogic science into a single clinical framework.

2020

Residential Practice - Shuddhi

Joined Shuddhi - one of India's largest Ayurveda and Naturopathy networks - as a resident practitioner across their Panchakarma and wellness centers. Gained deep clinical experience treating chronic lifestyle conditions through structured detoxification and root-cause protocols at scale.

2021

BNYS Completed

Completed the BNYS qualification following the mandatory clinical residency at Shuddhi. Formally registered with the STED Council, Government of India as a Naturopath and Ayurvedic Practitioner.

2022

500+ Lives Transformed

Crossed a milestone of over 500 individuals achieving medicine-free living through personalized root-cause healing protocols - with results spanning diabetes reversal, hormonal balance, gut health, and chronic pain elimination.
