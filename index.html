About

My goal is to treat the person - not the disease, not the system, not just the symptoms.

This is personalized medicine. Medicine of the future, practiced today.

As a wellbeing coach with deep training in Naturopathy and Ayurveda, I've spent over twelve years witnessing the same pattern: people trapped in cycles of medication, lifestyle diseases multiplying, hormonal and gut disorders becoming the norm.

I built a different approach. One that asks why before prescribing what. One focused on nutrition, circadian rhythm, gut health, and the daily habits that actually move the needle.

My mission is simple: empower you to heal yourself. Because lifelong dependence on medicines is not the answer.
