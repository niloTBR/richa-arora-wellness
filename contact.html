Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do virtual consultations work?

Virtual consultations are conducted via Zoom or Google Meet. You'll receive a link before your appointment. All you need is a stable internet connection and a quiet space.

What should I prepare for my first consultation?

Please have any recent lab reports ready, along with a list of current medications and supplements. A health questionnaire will be sent to you before the session.

Do you prescribe medications?

My approach focuses on natural interventions including diet, lifestyle, herbs, and supplements. I work alongside your existing healthcare providers when needed.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients notice improvements within 4-6 weeks. Complete transformation of chronic conditions typically takes 3-6 months of consistent effort.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, appointments can be rescheduled up to 24 hours before your session. Last-minute cancellations may be subject to a fee.

Do you work with people outside India?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide through virtual consultations. Sessions can be scheduled according to your timezone.
