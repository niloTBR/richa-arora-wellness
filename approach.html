Philosophy

Root. Not Symptom.

Most chronic conditions don't arise overnight. They develop over years of accumulated stress, poor nutrition, disrupted sleep, and unresolved imbalances. Treating them requires more than a prescription. It requires understanding the full story of how the body arrived at this point.

"Every system in the body is in constant dialogue with every other. Real healing begins when we listen to the whole conversation, not just the loudest symptom in the room."

Drawing on Naturopathy, Ayurveda, Yogic Sciences, and clinical nutrition, my practice looks at the whole person, not just the diagnosis on a report. When we understand why the body is struggling, the path to lasting health becomes clear.

My Philosophy

The Pillars of My Approach

Ayurvedic Wisdom

Ayurveda recognizes what modern medicine often misses: each person has a unique constitution, and what heals one may not heal another. By understanding your Prakriti and restoring balance, we work with your body's own intelligence, not against it.

Naturopathy

Naturopathy starts from a simple premise: the body wants to heal. My role is to remove what's blocking it. Through targeted nutrition, natural therapies, and root-cause investigation, we address disease at its source rather than its surface.

Lifestyle Medicine

Supplements and protocols can only go so far. Lasting health lives in daily choices - how we eat, sleep, move, and manage stress. This pillar focuses on building rhythms that sustain healing long after our sessions end.
